Bridging Traditional Wisdom with Modern Healthcare

AyushSetu is a unique coding system created to document patient information using traditional Indian medical knowledge, including symptoms, diagnosis, and treatments, in a way that respects cultural practices and aids healthcare equity efforts. While it captures the richness of traditional medicine, the challenge has always been to make this data understandable and usable on a global scale.

The Power of Integration

By converting Namaste Code entries into ICD-11 codes, we ensure patient information is standardized, globally compatible, and ready for research, reporting, and public health management.

How It Works

When a doctor records a patient's condition in Namaste Code, our system automatically maps it to the corresponding ICD-11 code. This integration creates a unified, efficient, and effective healthcare system.
